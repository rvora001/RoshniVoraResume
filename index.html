Hello! My name is Roshni Vora and I am a recent graduate from the University of California Riverside. I am an aspiring Environmentalist with a interest in sustainability and sustainable living. As a member of the Gen Z generation and a dedicated environmental advocate, I am determined to fight against those that are threatening the future of our planet. I think that if we all work together we can surely reverse the effects of climate change and live prosperly with the Earth.

From my recent years in undergrad I have learned a great deal about the environment and the effects of climate change. My focus has been in investing sustainable alternatives to otherwise wasteful and detrimental actions that are being done to our planet. Working with other companies and cooperations, I think we can all find easy ways we can decrease our carbon footprint and thus create a healthier environment for everyone.

My career goals that I hope to accomplish in the near future include learning more about ways we can increase economic growth that is in the best interest of our planet.
